_The Great Gatsby_

written by F. Scott Fitzgerald

, is a riveting story of love, wealth, and tragedy. Set during the glamorous Jazz Age of the 1920s, it follows the life of the mysterious and wealthy Jay Gatsby as he pursues his dream of reuniting with his lost love, Daisy Buchanan. The novel explores themes of decay, illusion, and the American Dream, offering a haunting portrait of a bygone era.
